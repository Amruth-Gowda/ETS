Employees performance tracking system (Web) 
------------------------------------------
Admin 
- manages staffs(creating accounts for staff)
	- create accounts
	- delete accounts

Manager
- Login
- Assign Projects to Team Leads(TL)
- will have the Track of the Projects
- Views the performances of TL and TM
 
Team Lead
- Login
- Assign Project Modules to Team Members(TM)
- will have the Track of the Modules
- Views the performances of TM

Staff
- Login
- Works on the assigned Tasks and 
- Updates about his progress of the task
